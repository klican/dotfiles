John Williams, classical guitar player, is known for his wide-ranging approach to repertory, which includes appearances playing electric rock guitar and international music. 
Williams has toured throughout the world, performed and recorded nearly the entire standard guitar repertory, plus a large quantity of transcriptions. Several of these transcriptions are by his own hand.
